— Who we are

Built by engineers.
Driven by purpose.

HydroGen was born from a simple belief: water is life, and technology can protect it. We're a team of graduates building the future of smart irrigation — one sensor at a time.

— Our mission

Making clean water accessible for every farmer

Egypt faces growing water scarcity challenges. Traditional irrigation wastes up to 60% of water through evaporation, runoff, and over-watering. We set out to change that.

HydroGen combines atmospheric water generation with AI-driven irrigation control — giving farmers a smarter, more sustainable way to grow. Our system works autonomously, learning from real-time sensor data to deliver exactly the right amount of water, exactly when it's needed.

We launched in 2026 as a graduation project, and we're proud of what we've built together.

— Our journey

How we got here

September 2025

The idea begins

Our team of 8 engineering students came together with one question: can we extract water from air and irrigate crops automatically?

November 2025

Hardware prototyping

We built our first ESP32-based sensor node, integrating temperature, humidity, and soil moisture sensors with real-time Firebase.

January 2026

Web dashboard launch

The HydroGen web platform went live — connecting hardware to a full-featured dashboard with AI irrigation recommendations and live analytics.

March 2026

AI integration complete

We integrated AI-driven pump control that automatically decides when to irrigate based on soil dryness, weather forecasts, and tank water levels.

April 2026

Graduation project submission

HydroGen was submitted as our graduation project — a fully functional smart irrigation system ready to make a real difference.
