What is a
smart
irrigation
system?

A smart irrigation system uses advanced technology to optimize water usage for agricultural or landscaping purposes. By integrating sensors, weather data, and automated controls, these systems ensure that plants receive the right amount of water at the right time, promoting healthy growth while conserving water resources.
